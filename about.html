Apolleon

derived from the greek god Apollo and acheion (ἄρχειον) the greek word for archive
Convent Sainte-Marie de La Tourette 1961 
Apolleon is an archive project started in September 2024.

It is a personal living collection of music, books and media
that I consume in life and also serves as a platform for me
to share recommendations and offer insights on certain media.

I will update this site as frequently as possible,
lastest update dates can be found on most pages.
